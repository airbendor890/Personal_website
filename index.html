Data Analysis

An essential aspect of every day life and business in various sectors is decision making. Human intuition is one of the best instantly available tool to draw some insight. But in todays information era the amount of data available is growing at exponential rate. Therefore using various tools and approaches to analyze these data have become an active topic. Sophisticated analysis of data can substantially improve decision making, minimize risks, and unearth valuable insights that would otherwise remain hidden. I often use Python and its libraries. These are great tools in data analysis for its simplicity, rich ecosystem of libraries in statistics and machine learning, open source and community support. When it comes to making dash boards, I look for microsoft Power BI.

Web development

Web technologies and tools are in constant track of improvements with some trending tools from time to time. Currently I am working with Boostrap, React and Node.js.

Physics

My favourite topic in physics is statistical mechanics which accounts for the macroscopic behaviour of physical systems in terms of dynamical laws governing the microscopic constituents of these systems and the probabilistic assumptions made about them. Its principle are employed in all other brances of physics. The quantity of interest is calculating partition function which often involves problem of counting.
